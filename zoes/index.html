About

Discover the epitome of posh sophistication and cozy luxury at Zoe’s Restaurant – the latest culinary gem in the North of England. With roots deeply embedded in the success of our acclaimed London establishments (The Landmark London Group), we bring boutique elegance and the expertise of top-notch chefs to our new branches. Immerse yourself in a culinary journey where every dish is a masterpiece, crafted with the finest locally sourced ingredients.

Our commitment to creating a warm and intimate ambiance makes Zoe’s Restaurant the perfect destination for those who appreciate a touch of northern hospitality blended seamlessly with urban chic. Join us and experience the art of fine dining in our newly established havens.

Our
chefs

Erin
Johnson

Erin Johnson, born and raised in the bustling culinary city of Savorville, developed an early passion for blending flavors and experimenting with diverse ingredients. Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of their hometown, Erin pursued formal culinary training and honed their skills in both classical and avant-garde cuisine.

Sandra
Rodriguez

Hailing from the sunny coastal town of Marisole, Sadra Rodrigez brings a touch of Latin flair to the culinary world. Growing up surrounded by the vibrant colors and flavors of seaside cuisine, Sadra's culinary journey began in the family kitchen. After mastering the art of blending spices and crafting authentic dishes, her path crossed with Zoe’s.

Tuna
Ekmekçi

Tuna Ekmekçi, a culinary prodigy from the historic city of Spiceburg, discovered a love for the culinary arts through the enchanting aromas that filled the bustling spice markets. Raised in a family of spice merchants, Tuna developed an innate understanding of how spices could elevate a dish to new heights.
